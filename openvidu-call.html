Your own videoconferencing app


Ready to use

OpenVidu Call is a ready to use, professional looking, videoconferencing application.

With all the features you expect: multiparty videoconferences, smart layouts, virtual background, recording, integrated chat, screen sharing...

Adapt it to your needs

It is implemented with an open source license, so you can adapt it to your needs. You have full control over the UI (brand, colors, icons...), you can decide which user is able to join each session, you can add recording, etc...

On your own servers

OpenVidu call is designed to be installed on premises in your own servers or in any cloud provider. It is available by default when you install OpenVidu platform.

Join with a click

You can join a session with a click. OpenVidu Call by default provides a random session name to provide safer access, but you can fully customize the name.

Inviting participants to a conference is as simple as sharing a URL.

Of course, you can modify OpenVidu Call to control how users enter to your sessions.

Setting up your session

OpenVidu Call provides different options and configurations for users before joining a session.

This includes testing and selecting audio and video devices, setting up a virtual background, choosing a nickname and changing the language.

Besides, these options are remembered by default by the client's device. If they refresh the page or join a different session, their previous configuration will be automatically applied.

Choose the perfect background effect

OpenVidu Call seamlessly integrates the Virtual Background feature.

Our background effects allow your users to participate in video conferences with a private or funny touch.

Recording your sessions for posterity

Now you don't have to worry about recordings, OpenVidu Call includes recordings as one of its basic features.

The session creator will be able to record the sessions and delete them in a very intuitive way inside of the Activities Panel.

Moreover, the others participants also will be able to play and download the video recordings once the recording is finished.

Multi-party videoconference

With OpenVidu Call you can join into a multi-party videoconference, displayed in a nice, intelligent layout. You will be able to zoom in, zoom out and fullscreen any video you want.

Toggling the participants panel (bottom right corner) you can check at a glance all the participants connected to the session.

Screen sharing in the blink of an eye

With OpenVidu Call you can share your screen and your webcam at the same time. And several users can do it simultaneously!

Thanks to our intelligent layout, your screen share video will take the lead and will be shown at a larger size than the rest, never being cropped so no information is lost.

Moreover, you can seamlessly switch your shared screen on the fly with the click of a button.

Chatting time!

OpenVidu Call also includes a nice chat already integrated in the intelligent layout.

The chat provides you an alternative way to silently exchange messages with all your videoconference partners. Everything works as you expect: messages are properly displayed with the author's name, links are automatically formatted to allow clicking on them, users will be subtly notified when new messages arrive, mobile view is specifically adapted for ease of use...

A great and advanced chat out-of-the-box!

Recording management in an admin dashboard

With OpenVidu Call you can handle the recording management in a very intuitive dashboard.

Not only you will be able to login and logout from the Admin Dashboard but you will also be able to preview the recordings, download them or delete those you don't need anymore. Just with a couple of clicks.
