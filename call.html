Your own videoconference app


Ready to use

OpenVidu Call is a ready to use, professional looking, videconference application.

With the features you expect: multiparty videoconference, smart layouts, chats, screen sharing...

Adapt it to your needs

It is implemented with an open source license, so you can adapt it to your needs. You have full control over graphical user interface (brand, colors, icons...), what user is able to enter to the every session, add recording, etc.

On your own servers

OpenVidu call is designed to be installed on your premises or on your preferred cloud provider. It is installed by default when you install OpenVidu platform.

Join with a click

You can join to room with a click. OpenVidu Call by default provide us a random session name with the aim of get safer the room access, but you can customize the name.

Invite your mates to a conference will be easier than ever. Just share the URL with whomever the user wants to join the videoconference.

You can change the application to control how users enter to media sessions.

Setting up your session

OpenVidu Call allows you configure your session before join to the room.

You not only will can check your audio and video devices before join to room, but also you will be able to switch them.

Besides, you can capture your own avatar and set your custom nickname.

Multi-party videoconference

With OpenVidu Call you can join into a multi-party videoconference, displayed in a nice and intelligent layout. You will be able to zoom in and zoom out and do fullscreen the video you want.

As we are observing at the botton of the video room, we can see all the remotes users connected to the session. This is an awesome information when an user is just publishing audio.

Screen sharing in the blink of an eye

With OpenVidu Call you will be able to share your desktop or presentations and webcam at the same time. And several users can do it.

Thanks to intelligent layout, your screen share video will take the lead and be shown at a larger size than the rest.

Moreover, you will can seamlessly switch your screen shared on the fly.

Chatting time!

OpenVidu Call also includes a nice chat integrated inside of the layout.

The integrated chat provides you an alternative way to exchange messages silently with all your videoconference mates.
