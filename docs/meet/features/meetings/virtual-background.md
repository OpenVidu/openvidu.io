# Virtual Background

Virtual backgrounds help participants present a cleaner, more professional video appearance without needing to change their physical environment.

With a single click, users can replace distracting surroundings with a **blur effect** or a **custom background image**, making meetings more comfortable, consistent, and privacy-friendly.

This feature works both before joining a meeting and while already inside one, giving users full flexibility at any moment.

Virtual backgrounds can be enabled or disabled per room when [creating](../rooms/management.md#create-rooms) or [editing a room](../rooms/management.md#edit-rooms), allowing administrator to decide when and where the feature is available.

## Key Benefits

- **More professional meetings** — reduce distractions and improve visual consistency across participants
- **Better privacy** — keep personal or sensitive environments out of view
- **No setup required** — works instantly with no external tools or configuration
- **Flexible control** — enable or disable per room depending on the use case

## Available Effects

### Blur

Quickly hide your surroundings while keeping the focus on you.

- **Soft blur** — subtle background reduction for a natural look
- **Hard blur** — stronger isolation for maximum privacy

### Image Backgrounds

Replace your environment with a ready-to-use image selected to match different meeting contexts:

- **Professional** — clean, formal environments for business meetings
- **Home Office** — comfortable, remote-work friendly settings
- **Creative** — expressive backgrounds for more informal or dynamic sessions

## Using Virtual Background

### Before joining

1. Open the virtual background panel in the device preview screen
2. Select a blur level or background image — changes apply instantly in the preview
3. Your last selection is automatically remembered for future meetings

### During a meeting

1. Open the **More Options** menu in the toolbar
2. Select **Virtual Background**
3. Choose an effect to apply it instantly
